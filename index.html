I have been a professional freelance musician (flute and piano) for over a decade. I have played flute for a national Windex ad, performed with Yo-Yo Ma, Jewel, and Johnny Mathis, and have performed all over the world, including with the Lyric Opera of Chicago, Milwaukee Symphony, Hawaii Opera, and Hawaii Symphony. Despite having a passion to perform and teach, the lack of security in this field pushed me to move my life in a different direction...and possibly a better one, ultimately.

After doing a bit of research (by Googling, ironically enough) I discovered web development and Wyncode.

As part of the week 3 curriculum at Wyncode Academy, I am putting up this website using HTML and CSS. The first two weeks focused on learning and completing projects using Ruby. Please feel free to click the above "Projects" button to check out the various projects I have pushed to Github. After Ruby, we covered Internet protocols and had two days of lectures on HTML and CSS. This website was created over one weekend to get practical experience using these technologies and to have a site to show off what we've learned. I will add more as the weeks progress. Stay tuned!

At the end of the course, we will have a "Pitch Day", pitching our final project to friends, family, and potential hiring partners. This project will be included in my Github profile.
